      研究理念 · About Research

      Research Question

      同样一组命理信息，为何会因性别、文化背景与社会期待而产生截然不同的解释？

      Project Motivation

      命理语言承载千年文化，也隐含性别偏见与恐惧叙事。

      Methodology

      1. 采集传统命理解读文本（模拟数据集）
      2. 标注叙事偏见类别（七维框架）
      3. AI 辅助生成去偏见重构版本
      4. 用户参与式三方对话（学者 / AI / 自我）
      5. 渐进式偏见碎片收集机制

      Bias Categories

      • 性别偏见
      • 婚姻中心论
      • 命运决定论
      • 恐惧叙事
      • 道德审判
      • 异性恋默认
      • 阶级叙事

      References

      • Butler, J. (1990). Gender Trouble. Routledge.
      • Foucault, M. (1980). Power/Knowledge. Pantheon.
      • Haraway, D. (1988). Situated Knowledges. Feminist Studies, 14(3).
      • Adorno, T. & Horkheimer, M. (1944). Dialectic of Enlightenment.
      • Tu, W. (1985). Confucian Thought: Selfhood as Creative Transformation.

      高中生研究项目 · Reframe Destiny © 2025

      Reframe Destiny — Research

      Research Question

      How does an AI website help young people (15–35, men and women) find and reframe gender bias in BaZi and astrology?

      Hypothesis

      After using Reframe Destiny, users will notice gendered narrative bias more and feel confident to rewrite their stories.

      Why it matters

      The same chart can mean "leader" for men but "too strong" for women. We study gender bias in divination narratives, not whether divination is true.

      Method (planned)

      15–25 young people (15–35) · pre/post survey · 7-step website · user study not run yet.

      Progress

      Prototype done · research/ folder done · Week 2–3 deliverables on GitHub.
