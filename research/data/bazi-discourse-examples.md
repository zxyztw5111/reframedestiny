# BaZi Discourse Examples

Purpose: keep examples of gendered divination language for later qualitative coding. These are raw materials, not academic proof.

## Example themes to code

- "克夫" / husband-restraining labels attached to women
- Strong personality described as marriage risk for women
- Husband or marriage treated as the central measure of female destiny
- Female ambition reframed as emotional or relational danger
- Modern reinterpretations that challenge the traditional label

## Coding plan

For each collected passage, code:

- Source and URL
- Exact quoted phrase
- Bias category
- Who is being judged
- What life outcome is being valued
- Whether the passage is traditional, modernized, or critical
