> **Gap:** No large representative survey found with head-to-head % trust (AI vs human master). Below is qualitative + proxy data.

## 8. Key gaps for user study / paper

1. No published **representative** China national survey with clean **15–35** age band and **BaZi vs tarot vs astrology** breakdown.
2. No **quantitative** AI-vs-human trust comparison — design opportunity for MVP evaluation.
3. Market size estimates vary **~100×** (¥22B vs ¥120B vs ¥680B+) depending on category definition (AI-only vs divination services vs "好运经济" retail).
4. Gender data strong for **US (Pew)** and suggestive for **China (industry surveys)** but cross-cultural comparison needs caution.
